Understanding Coney Island Aquarium Hours and Rates

Exploring Coney Island Aquarium Hours and Rates

When it comes to rates, the Coney Island Aquarium offers a variety of ticket options. General admission for adults is $29.95, while children aged 3-12 can enter for $24.95.
