CML Philadelphia Chromosome Treatment: What is it?

CML Philadelphia chromosome treatment is a targeted therapy used to manage Chronic Myeloid Leukemia (CML). CML is a type of cancer that affects the bone marrow and blood. It is characterized by the presence of a genetic abnormality known as the Philadelphia chromosome, which leads to the overproduction of abnormal white blood cells.

The goal of CML Philadelphia chromosome treatment is to inhibit the activity of the BCR-ABL protein, which is produced by the Philadelphia chromosome. By targeting this protein, CML Philadelphia chromosome treatment can help control the growth of abnormal cells and prevent the progression of the disease.
