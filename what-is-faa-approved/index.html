What is FAA approved?

When we say something is FAA approved, it means that it has gone through a rigorous evaluation process conducted by the FAA and has been found to meet the necessary safety requirements. This can apply to various aspects of aviation, such as aircraft, avionics, maintenance procedures, training programs, and more. Essentially, the FAA approval signifies that a product or service meets the highest safety standards set by the FAA.
