Philadelphia Cream Cheese Cheesecake Recipe with Sour Cream

I recently had the pleasure of trying out this Philadelphia Cream Cheese Cheesecake recipe with sour cream, and let me tell you, it did not disappoint. The recipe was easy to follow, and the end result was a creamy and delicious cheesecake that had everyone going back for seconds.

To start, you'll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 3 packages (8 ounces each) Philadelphia Cream Cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

First, preheat your oven to 325Â°F and prepare your graham cracker crust. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs and melted butter until well mixed. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan and set aside.

Next, in a large mixing bowl, beat the softened cream cheese until smooth and creamy. Add in the sour cream, sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract, and continue to beat until well combined and smooth. Pour the mixture into the prepared crust.

Bake the cheesecake for 55-60 minutes, or until the center is set and the top is lightly golden. Remove from the oven and let cool completely before refrigerating for at least 4 hours or overnight.

Once chilled, remove the cheesecake from the springform pan and transfer to a serving plate. You can serve it plain or top it with your favorite fruit topping or whipped cream.
