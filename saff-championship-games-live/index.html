SAFF Championship Games Live: A Closer Look at the Tournament

The SAFF Championship Games Live is a biennial football tournament organized by the South Asian Football Federation. The tournament brings together teams from eight South Asian countries, including India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Nepal, Maldives, Bhutan, and Afghanistan.
