CT Brain Scan Cost in Hyderabad: What is a CT Brain Scan?

A CT brain scan, also known as a computed tomography scan, is a medical imaging procedure that uses X-rays and computer technology to create detailed cross-sectional images of the brain. It is a non-invasive and painless procedure that can help diagnose various conditions, such as tumors, bleeding, infections, and abnormalities in the brain.

During the CT brain scan, the patient lies on a table that slides into a large, donut-shaped machine. X-ray beams are directed through the head, and the detectors in the machine measure the amount of radiation that passes through the brain. The data is then processed by a computer to create detailed images that can be viewed by a radiologist or physician.

Q: How long does a CT brain scan take?

A: The actual scanning time for a CT brain scan is usually less than a minute. However, the overall procedure may take longer due to preparation and post-processing of the images.

Q: Is a CT brain scan safe?

A: Yes, CT brain scans are generally considered safe. However, they do involve exposure to radiation. The amount of radiation used is kept as low as possible to minimize any potential risks.

Q: Do I need a referral for a CT brain scan?

A: It depends on the facility and your insurance coverage. Some facilities may require a referral from a healthcare professional, while others may allow self-referral. It is best to check with the facility beforehand.

Q: How should I prepare for a CT brain scan?
