The History and Myth of Luna Park Coney Island

Luna Park Coney Island has a fascinating history that dates back over a century. It was one of three major amusement parks that once graced Coney Island, along with Dreamland and Steeplechase Park. Luna Park was known for its extravagant architecture, dazzling lights, and innovative rides.
