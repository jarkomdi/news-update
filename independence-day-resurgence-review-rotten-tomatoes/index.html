History and Myth of Independence Day Resurgence Review Rotten Tomatoes

Rotten Tomatoes was founded in 1998 by Senh Duong and Patrick Y. Lee. It started as a hobby for Duong, who wanted to create a website that would aggregate movie reviews and provide a score based on the aggregated reviews. The name "Rotten Tomatoes" is derived from the practice of throwing rotten tomatoes at performers to express disapproval.

Over the years, Rotten Tomatoes has become one of the most popular review aggregator websites.
