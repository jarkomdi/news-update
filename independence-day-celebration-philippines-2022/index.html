The History and Myth of Independence Day Celebration Philippines 2022

The history of Independence Day in the Philippines dates back to June 12, 1898, when General Emilio Aguinaldo declared the country's independence from Spain. This declaration came after years of resistance and revolution against Spanish colonial rule. The myth surrounding Independence Day is that the Philippine flag was first raised in Kawit, Cavite, where General Aguinaldo proclaimed the country's independence.

However, historical records suggest that the first Philippine flag was actually unfurled in May 1898 in Alapan, Imus, Cavite, during the Battle of Alapan. The myth of Kawit being the birthplace of the Philippine flag persists due to its association with General Aguinaldo and the significance of the proclamation of independence in Kawit.
