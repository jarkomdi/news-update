Question and Answer

1. What is the best time to visit Koh Samui?

The best time to visit Koh Samui is during the dry season, which typically runs from December to April. This period offers sunny weather and less rainfall, making it ideal for outdoor activities and beach relaxation.

2. Can I find budget-friendly accommodations in Koh Samui?

Yes, Koh Samui offers a range of budget-friendly accommodations, including guesthouses, hostels, and affordable hotels. These options provide comfortable and convenient stays without breaking the bank.
