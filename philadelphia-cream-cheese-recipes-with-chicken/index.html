To make this recipe, start by seasoning your chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat and cook the chicken breasts until they are browned and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside. In the same skillet, melt some butter and add minced garlic. Cook the garlic for a minute or until fragrant. Then, add Philadelphia cream cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, chicken broth, and heavy cream to the skillet. Stir until the cream cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth. Return the chicken breasts to the skillet and simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens. Serve the creamy garlic Parmesan chicken with pasta or rice and enjoy!
