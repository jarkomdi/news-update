What is Independence Day Tagalog Meaning?

Independence Day in the Philippines, known as "Araw ng Kalayaan" in Tagalog, is a national holiday that commemorates the country's declaration of independence from Spanish colonial rule on June 12, 1898. It is a day of great importance and pride for Filipinos, as it symbolizes their freedom and sovereignty.

History and Myth of Independence Day Tagalog Meaning

The struggle for independence in the Philippines began long before the actual declaration in 1898. Filipinos had been fighting for their freedom from Spanish colonization for centuries, with notable figures such as Jose Rizal and Andres Bonifacio leading the movement. The myth surrounding the flag and the declaration of independence adds to the significance of this day.
