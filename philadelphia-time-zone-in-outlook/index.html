Question and Answer

Q: How do I change the time zone in Outlook?
A: To change the time zone in Outlook, go to File > Options > Calendar > Time zones. From there, you can select the desired time zone and adjust the settings accordingly.

Q: Does Outlook automatically adjust for daylight saving time?
A: Yes, Outlook automatically adjusts for daylight saving time based on the specified time zone. However, it is still important to double-check your settings to ensure accuracy.

Q: Can I set multiple time zones in my Outlook calendar?
A: Yes, you can set multiple time zones in your Outlook calendar. Simply go to File > Options > Calendar > Time zones and add the desired time zones to your calendar view.

Q: Are there any shortcuts or hotkeys for time zone adjustments in Outlook?
A: Yes, you can use the "Ctrl + Shift + W" shortcut to switch between time zones in Outlook. This can be helpful when quickly comparing meeting times across different locations.
