What is Fourth of July 2023 Federal Holiday?

The Fourth of July 2023 Federal Holiday, also known as Independence Day, commemorates the adoption of the Declaration of Independence on July 4, 1776. This document declared the thirteen American colonies independent from British rule and marked the birth of the United States of America. The Fourth of July has since become a national holiday and is celebrated with parades, fireworks, picnics, and other festive activities. It is a day to honor the history and values of our nation and to express pride in being an American.

History and Myth of Fourth of July 2023 Federal Holiday

The Fourth of July has a rich history and is steeped in myth and tradition. Contrary to popular belief, the signing of the Declaration of Independence did not actually occur on July 4th. The document was adopted by the Second Continental Congress on July 2nd, and the final version was approved and signed on August 2nd. However, July 4th became the date associated with the holiday because it was the day that the Continental Congress formally declared independence from Britain. The Fourth of July has since been celebrated as a day of freedom and independence.
