What is the Wimbledon Tennis Tickets Ballot?

The Wimbledon Tennis Tickets Ballot is a lottery system that allows tennis fans to enter for a chance to purchase tickets for matches at the Wimbledon Championships. The objective of the ballot is to provide a fair and equitable way for fans to access tickets, as demand for Wimbledon tickets far exceeds supply.

The Wimbledon Tennis Tickets Ballot has been in operation since the 1920s and has become an integral part of the Wimbledon experience.
