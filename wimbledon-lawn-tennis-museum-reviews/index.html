What is Wimbledon Lawn Tennis Museum?

The Wimbledon Lawn Tennis Museum is a dedicated museum located at the All England Lawn Tennis and Croquet Club in London. It houses an extensive collection of tennis memorabilia and artifacts, showcasing the rich history and tradition of the sport. The museum offers a variety of exhibits, interactive displays, and educational programs, making it a must-visit destination for tennis enthusiasts and sports fans.

The museum's collection includes items such as championship trophies, vintage tennis rackets, and iconic outfits worn by tennis legends. Visitors can also explore the evolution of the sport through interactive exhibits that showcase technological advancements and the changing rules of the game. The museum provides a comprehensive and immersive experience that brings the history of tennis to life.

The Wimbledon Lawn Tennis Museum has a rich history that dates back to its founding in 1977.
