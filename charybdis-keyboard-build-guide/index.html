A Charybdis keyboard is a custom keyboard that is built from scratch using individual components. Recommendations for Building a Charybdis Keyboard

When it comes to building a Charybdis keyboard, there are a few key components and tools that you will need. Choosing the Right Switches

One of the most important decisions you will make when building a Charybdis keyboard is choosing the right switches. Switches determine how your keyboard feels and sounds when you type. There are three main types of switches: linear, tactile, and clicky. Linear switches have a smooth and consistent feel, while tactile switches provide a slight bump when pressed. Clicky switches, as the name suggests, produce an audible click when pressed. Consider your typing style and preferences when choosing switches.
