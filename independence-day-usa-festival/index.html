What is Independence Day USA Festival?

Independence Day USA Festival, also known as the Fourth of July, is a national holiday in the United States that commemorates the signing of the Declaration of Independence on July 4, 1776. It is a day to celebrate the birth of our nation and the freedom we enjoy as Americans. The festival is marked by various events and traditions, including fireworks displays, parades, concerts, barbecues, and family gatherings.

The history of Independence Day USA Festival dates back to the American Revolutionary War, when the thirteen colonies declared their independence from British rule. Over the years, the holiday has evolved to include various traditions and customs that celebrate the values and ideals of our nation.
