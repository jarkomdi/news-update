Fun Facts About the Philippine Day of Independence

Did you know that:

- The Philippines is the first country in Southeast Asia to gain independence from a colonial power.

- The Philippine flag was first unfurled on June 12, 1898, during the declaration of independence.

- Emilio Aguinaldo, the first President of the Philippines, proclaimed the country's independence from the balcony of his ancestral home in Kawit, Cavite.

- The Philippine Day of Independence was originally celebrated on July 4th, but it was changed to June 12th in 1964 to coincide with the date of the declaration of independence.
