What is Zombie Zone Meaning?

Zombie zone meaning refers to a state of mind in which a person is completely disconnected from their surroundings. It is a condition characterized by a lack of awareness, focus, and engagement with the present moment. When someone is in the zombie zone, they are essentially operating on autopilot, going through the motions without truly being present. This state is often associated with feelings of numbness, emptiness, and a sense of detachment from reality.
