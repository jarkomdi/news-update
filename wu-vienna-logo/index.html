What is the WU Vienna Logo?

The History and Myth of the WU Vienna Logo

The WU Vienna logo was created in 2004 as part of a rebranding effort by the university. The previous logo, which had been in use since the establishment of the university in 1898, was seen as outdated and no longer aligned with the institution's vision and values.

The new logo was designed to reflect WU Vienna's transformation into a modern and innovative institution. It was meant to convey a sense of dynamism, progress, and openness to change.
