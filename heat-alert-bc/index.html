During periods of high heat, it is important to take extra precautions to stay safe and prevent heat-related illnesses. Some tips for staying safe during high heat include:

1. Stay hydrated:

2. Find cool places:

Seek out air-conditioned spaces, such as malls, libraries, or community centers, to escape the heat. If you do not have access to air conditioning, consider visiting public places with air conditioning, such as shopping centers or movie theaters.

3. Dress appropriately:

Wear loose-fitting, lightweight, and light-colored clothing to help your body regulate its temperature. Avoid dark colors, as they absorb more heat.

4. Limit outdoor activities:

Try to limit outdoor activities, especially during the hottest parts of the day. If you must be outside, take frequent breaks in shaded areas and avoid strenuous activities.
