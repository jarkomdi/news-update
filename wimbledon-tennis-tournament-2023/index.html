Wimbledon Tennis Tournament 2023 is one of the four Grand Slam tournaments in professional tennis. It takes place annually at the All England Lawn Tennis and Croquet Club in Wimbledon, London. Wimbledon is unique in that it is the only Grand Slam tournament that is played on grass courts. The tournament is known for its strict dress code, with players required to wear all-white attire.
