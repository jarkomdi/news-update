Tips for Perfect Byrdie Nails

1. Start with a clean canvas - Make sure to remove any old polish and gently file your nails to your desired shape before applying any products.

2. Use a base coat - Applying a base coat before your nail polish helps to protect your natural nails from staining and creates a smooth surface for better polish adhesion.

3. Choose the right tools - Invest in high-quality nail brushes, files, and cuticle pushers to ensure precise application and a professional finish.

4. Take care of your cuticles - Regularly moisturize and gently push back your cuticles to promote healthy nail growth and prevent hangnails.

5. Seal the deal - Finish off your manicure with a top coat to protect your polish and add shine and longevity to your nails.
