The Too Good To Go app UK is a platform that connects users with local businesses that have surplus food. These businesses include restaurants, cafes, bakeries, and grocery stores. Users can then browse through the available options, make a purchase, and collect their food during the specified pickup time. It's a simple and convenient way to save money and reduce food waste.
