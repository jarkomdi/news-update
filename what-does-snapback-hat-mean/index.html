What Does Snapback Hat Mean?

A snapback hat is a type of baseball cap that has an adjustable strap at the back. The strap consists of a row of plastic snaps, which allow the wearer to adjust the size of the hat to fit their head comfortably. The word "snapback" refers to the snapping mechanism of the strap, which makes it easy to snap the hat on and off.

While snapback hats were initially popularized by sports teams and athletes, they have now become a fashion statement and are worn by people of all ages and backgrounds. They are known for their flat brim, high crown, and structured design, which give them a unique and stylish look.

Now that we know what snapback hats are, let's take a closer look at their history and the myths associated with them.
