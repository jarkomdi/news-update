Understanding Caloric Intake and its Impact

Caloric intake refers to the number of calories consumed through food and beverages. Calories are a unit of energy, and our bodies require a certain amount of energy to function properly. Consuming more calories than we need can lead to weight gain, while consuming too few calories can result in weight loss and nutrient deficiencies.
